*Purushartha* is derived from *Purush* (a rational being, moral actor) and *Artha* (purpose, object of desire). It represents the pursuit of a meaningful life, guided by moral and spiritual values.  The concept rests on two beliefs:

* The universe operates with a purpose (teleological). [ğŸ”‰](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mzeSCpZCIrI?t=192)

Purushartha provides a framework for actions benefiting the individual, society, and the cosmos.  It offers a systematic approach to achieving a fulfilling and virtuous life, especially necessary in an imperfect world.  > "Purushartha is a moral and spiritual goal for humans."
