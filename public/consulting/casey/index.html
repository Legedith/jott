What is the BCG Online Case Study? ğŸ”‰

The BCG Online Case Study is a virtual case interview conducted through an interactive chatbot platform. This assessment aims to evaluate candidates’ ability to think like consultants by analyzing business scenarios, formulating solutions, and communicating effectively.

Key Assessment Criteria:

  • Business Judgment: Ability to understand and analyze business problems.
  • Logical Thinking: Ability to solve problems logically and systematically.
  • Comfort with Numbers: Proficiency in working with and interpreting data. ğŸ”‰

Format and Structure:

  • The assessment is conducted on a platform resembling Facebook Messenger, where you interact with a chatbot named Casey.
  • You have 25 minutes to complete 8-10 questions. ğŸ”‰
  • Questions are categorized into three types: ğŸ”‰
    • Multiple Choice Questions: Choose the correct answer from four options based on provided information.
    • Open-Ended Questions: Explain your reasoning for the chosen multiple-choice answer. ğŸ”‰
    • Redirection or Follow-up Questions: These questions may present additional information or challenge your previous answers.

Final Video Recommendation:

  • After completing the questions, you will be asked to record a one-minute video summarizing your findings and providing a recommendation based on the case scenario.
  • You have one minute to prepare and one minute to record. ğŸ”‰

Important Notes:

  • No Backtracking: You cannot revisit previous questions. ğŸ”‰
  • Allowed Resources: Calculators, pens, and paper are permitted during the assessment. ğŸ”‰
  • Time Warnings: Casey will give a five-minute warning before the test ends. ğŸ”‰

  • Gross Profit: Obtain the gross profit per liter from the table. ğŸ”‰
  • Weighted Average Price: Calculate the weighted average price per liter by multiplying each price range by its corresponding sales percentage.
  • Calculate Gross Margin: Divide the gross profit per liter by the weighted average price. Round to one decimal place.
